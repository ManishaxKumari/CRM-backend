List Organization Tips
Naming Strategy

Use clear, descriptive names that instantly tell you what the list contains. Avoid generic names like "List 1".

Purpose-Driven

Create lists based on specific purposes: projects, topics, or time periods for better organization.

Keep It Simple

Don't create too many lists. Start with 3-5 main categories and expand only when needed.

Review Regularly

Periodically review and update your lists. Archive old lists and create new ones as your needs change.
